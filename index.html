Cybera & Callysto
April 2019

It's clich√©, but knowledge is power. Through the use of Jupyter notebooks, my cross-country team of students, researchers, and professors created interactive lessons for students in middle school through university. The mission was to develop a wealth of teaching resources that emphasize computer science technologies, employing Python, HTML, and other coding languages. Utilizing a weekly SCRUM development cycle, we individually designed dozens of fascinating notebooks. The program was completed in association with the University of Calgary and the Pacific Institute for the Math Sciences (PIMS), as well as CanCode.
