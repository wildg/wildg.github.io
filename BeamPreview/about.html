Our

Information

We at Beam Creative Design strive to combine creativity with cutting technology offering tailored design solutions to our clients globally.

Our Beginning

Established in 2019 by Dr. Saeed Zaki, an architect, urban designer and urban planner, we bring over 30 years of research, planning and design knowledge in urban design, architecture and interior design.

Our Knowledge

Our team of local and international design professions have delivered award winning design projects in hospitality, commercial and residential spaces in Asia Pacific and beyond.

Our Commitment

To create memorable design outcome for our clients without compromising our commitment to sustainable development that respects context, culture and environment.

Technology

Building Information Modelling (BIM) is the tool of choice for our design and documentation ensuring cost and quality control through interdisciplinary coordination.
We work on latest cloud based platform allowing seamless collaboration between our Clients, design teams, other consultants and project managers.
